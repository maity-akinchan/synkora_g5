# Video Meeting - User Guide

## Quick Start

### Starting a Meeting
1. Open any project
2. Look at the **left sidebar**
3. Click the **"Start Meeting"** button (with video icon)
4. The meeting window will open
5. Allow camera and microphone permissions when prompted
6. You're in the meeting!

### Joining a Meeting
1. Open the same project
2. Click **"Start Meeting"** button
3. You'll automatically join the ongoing meeting
4. Other participants will see you join

### Leaving a Meeting
1. Click the **"Leave"** or **"End Call"** button in the meeting window
2. Or close the meeting modal
3. You'll be removed from the meeting

## Meeting Controls

### Video & Audio
- **Camera Icon**: Toggle your camera on/off
- **Microphone Icon**: Mute/unmute yourself
- **Settings Icon**: Adjust video/audio quality

### Screen Sharing
- **Screen Share Icon**: Share your screen
- **Stop Sharing**: Click again to stop

### Chat
- **Chat Icon**: Open text chat sidebar
- **Send Messages**: Type and send messages to all participants
- **Chat History**: See all messages during the meeting

### Participants
- **User List Icon**: See all participants
- **Participant Count**: Shows in the UI
- **Join/Leave Notifications**: See when people join or leave

### Layout Options
- **Grid View**: See everyone equally
- **Speaker View**: Focus on active speaker
- **Sidebar View**: Main speaker + thumbnails
- **Auto**: Automatically switches based on activity

## Best Practices

### Before the Meeting
1. **Test your equipment**: Check camera and microphone
2. **Good lighting**: Face a light source
3. **Quiet environment**: Minimize background noise
4. **Stable internet**: 5+ Mbps recommended
5. **Close unnecessary apps**: Free up bandwidth

### During the Meeting
1. **Mute when not speaking**: Reduce background noise
2. **Use headphones**: Prevent echo
3. **Good camera angle**: Eye level, centered
4. **Professional background**: Or use virtual background
5. **Stay engaged**: Keep camera on when possible

### Meeting Etiquette
1. **Be on time**: Join at scheduled time
2. **Introduce yourself**: When joining
3. **Raise hand**: Before speaking (if feature enabled)
4. **Stay focused**: Minimize distractions
5. **End properly**: Say goodbye before leaving

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Start Meeting
- Check if you're logged in
- Verify you have access to the project
- Check browser console for errors
- Refresh the page

### No Video/Audio
- **Grant Permissions**: Allow camera/microphone in browser
- **Check Device**: Ensure camera/mic are working
- **Try Different Browser**: Some browsers work better
- **Restart Browser**: Close and reopen

### Poor Connection
- **Check Internet**: Run speed test
- **Close Apps**: Free up bandwidth
- **Turn Off Video**: Audio-only uses less bandwidth
- **Move Closer to Router**: Improve WiFi signal

### Can't Hear Others
- **Check Volume**: Increase system volume
- **Check Browser**: Unmute browser tab
- **Ask Them to Unmute**: They might be muted
- **Try Headphones**: Better audio quality

### Echo or Feedback
- **Use Headphones**: Prevents speaker feedback
- **Mute When Not Speaking**: Reduces echo
- **Check Settings**: Adjust audio settings
- **Move Away from Speakers**: Reduce feedback loop

## Tips & Tricks

### Better Video Quality
1. **Good Lighting**: Face a window or lamp
2. **Clean Background**: Professional appearance
3. **Stable Camera**: Don't move around too much
4. **Eye Level**: Position camera at eye level

### Better Audio Quality
1. **Use Headphones**: Clearer audio, no echo
2. **External Microphone**: Better than built-in
3. **Quiet Room**: Close windows, turn off fans
4. **Speak Clearly**: Face the microphone

### Efficient Meetings
1. **Have an Agenda**: Know what to discuss
2. **Time Limit**: Keep meetings focused
3. **Take Notes**: Use chat or external notes
4. **Action Items**: Assign tasks after meeting
5. **Follow Up**: Send summary after meeting
